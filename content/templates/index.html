Dragonfly uses data to help government, industry, and business make effective decisions

Dragonfly prepared a summary of New Zealand's regional economic activity for the Ministry of Business, Innovation, & Employment. Photograph of Port Napier CC-BY Phillip Capper.

We use statistical analysis so our clients can make decisions based on the best available information. We are independent, and we work with our clients in an open and engaged way.

Our speciality is modern Bayesian analysis, which allows uncertainty to be carried all the way through from analysis to decision making. Bayesian modelling may be applied to a wide range of analytical problems, allowing best use of limited information, or to properly account for variation in data-rich situations. We also use a wide range of machine-learning and simulation methods, as required by our clients.

Our work is supported by excellent technical database and software-development skills. We build operation systems that allow organisations to get ongoing value from their data. Systems for data collection, management, nalysis, and reporting.
